Pasta

Description

This recipe is about a type of oil pasta with the main ingredient of garlic. Usually made with spagetti.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • spagetti
  • parsley
  • peperoncinno
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil enough water in a pot
  2. Add the pasta and a bit of salt to the boiling water
  3. While the pasta is boiling, slice the garlic
  4. 6 minutes after adding the pasta, add olive oil, garlic, and peperoncinno to a pan
  5. Cook them in low fire until the garlic turns gold
  6. When 10 minutes has passed, add the pasta and one scoop of water to the pan
  7. Cook until the water evaporates
